What is Project Retrowave?

Project Retrowave is the codename for my upcoming side-project(in alpha phase currently). Meaning, it has been only shared/released to a specific group of people either through an online group/collection or I personally know you.

Retrowave is a music player for YouTube, that only searches it's music category and provides you the latest/trending music related to your search that you can play and listen to, without going to YouTube.

So it is like Streamus?

Yes! With the basic feature of being able to search and play music and planned features of Custom and Curated Playlists, the concept is same as Streamus and all other YouTube music players out there.

Why make the same thing again?

youtu.be/kJa2kwoZ2a4

Streamus was the only YouTube music player which I feel had a very good UI, experience and features overall. It's discontinuation was a huge bummer. All the other players that are available(and I know of) either do not have have a good interface or feels incomplete in terms of features and experience they provide. So I decided to try and make one, myself.

What's the plan?

Well, good ol' alpha, beta, gamma(that's for Hulk) final release.

To elaborate, I will release a few(or a lot) alpha's based on your feedback*/s, fix bugs that I find and those reported by you. Jump to beta when I think it's ready for a broader group of people, with the same feedback* and bug fix loop and then put out the final stable release to the interwebs!

I am not sure what feedback* to provide, help!

No worries! If you do not have anything to say, I get it, relax! You are not obliged to provide a feedback*, it's totally upto you. Hope you enjoy using it tho :)

If you do plan to provide a feedback* and report bugs however, use the above tabs, to switch to the "feedback*" form and use that to provide a feedback* on features and improvements you would like to see or report bug/s you found.

The following are the features that I have roughly planned:

  • Curated Homescreen of Trending Playlists and Songs.
  • Custom user created Playlits.
  • Favorite a Song.
  • No-Signup Playlists, History, Favorites etc.

I "might" get a lot of unique and awesome feature requests from you guys, so I cannot gurantee about the implementation of each and every one of them, but I will definitely take a look at all of them and check which are the one's that work the best.

I like this project and want my friends to know about it.

Feel free to do so. However, keep in mind that this is the first alpha release, which means it will have a lot, I mean REALLY A LOT of bugs and therefore it is not suitable for a large group of people to use it as a stable release. Keeping that in mind, make sure to share this alpha project as at your own discretion.
